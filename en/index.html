About PGA

About Pathway Global Alliance

Pathway Global Alliance (PGA) is an international platform dedicated to strengthening connections between India and the world through structured global engagement. The organization serves as a bridge between nations by fostering collaboration among youth leaders, policymakers, entrepreneurs, athletes, cultural ambassadors, and institutions.

With a growing international footprint, PGA facilitates cross-border dialogue, people-to-people engagement, and strategic partnerships that promote mutual understanding and shared progress.
