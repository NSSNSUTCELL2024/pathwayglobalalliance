Shaheed-e-Aazam Bhagat Singh Memorial India-Russia-Uzbekistan Wrestling Cup

Distinguished Delegates and Dignitaries

The Shaheed-e-Aazam Bhagat Singh Memorial India-Russia-Uzbekistan Wrestling Cup was a landmark international sporting event organised by Pathway Global Alliance, bringing together wrestlers and dignitaries from India, Russia, and Uzbekistan to honour the legacy of the legendary freedom fighter Shaheed-e-Aazam Bhagat Singh.

The event was graced by distinguished delegates and dignitaries, including the Deputy Prime Minister of the Republic of Bashkortostan, Russia, who attended as a guest of honour, underscoring the strong ties between India and Russia in sports and cultural diplomacy. Among the notable attendees was Shanky Singh, the celebrated ex-WWE wrestler, whose presence brought immense excitement and inspiration to the event, highlighting the global reach of Indian wrestling talent.

About the Event

The wrestling cup served as a platform for fostering international sportsmanship and cultural exchange among the three nations. Wrestlers from India, Russia, and Uzbekistan competed in a spirit of camaraderie and mutual respect, paying tribute to the ideals of courage and sacrifice embodied by Bhagat Singh.

The event featured high-level delegations from all three countries, with senior officials, sports administrators, and cultural representatives coming together to strengthen bilateral and trilateral cooperation in the field of sports.
