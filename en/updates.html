Trination Wrestling Championship 2025

The "Shaheed-E-Azam Bhagat Singh Memorial India-Russia-Uzbekistan Belt Wrestling Cup 2025" took place at Maharshi Dayanand University (MDU) in Rohtak, Haryana. This tri-nation event featured athletes from India, Russia, and Uzbekistan competing in traditional belt wrestling, fostering international sportsmanship and cultural exchange.
