Nepal Visit

World Youth Forum — Nepal Visit

Strengthening Youth Ties Between India and Nepal

The Pathway Global Alliance delegation undertook a significant visit to Nepal as part of the World Youth Forum initiative. The visit focused on fostering cross-border youth engagement, cultural exchange, and strengthening diplomatic ties between India and Nepal through people-to-people contact.

During the visit, the delegation participated in meetings with local youth leaders, community organisations, and cultural institutions. The programme included interactive sessions, cultural presentations, and collaborative discussions aimed at building lasting partnerships for youth empowerment across the region.
