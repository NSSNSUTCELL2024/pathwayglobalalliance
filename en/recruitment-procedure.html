Recruitment Procedure

Our comprehensive recruitment procedure ensures we connect you with the ideal talent. We meticulously evaluate candidates based on a range of criteria.

1. Essential Qualifications

We verify educational credentials and ensure candidates possess the necessary qualifications for the role.

2. Relevant Work Experience

We thoroughly review work history, assessing the depth and relevance of experience to the specific job requirements.

3. Industrial Training

We confirm participation in relevant industrial training programs, ensuring candidates have practical skills and hands-on experience.

4. Trade-Specific Courses & Certifications

We validate trade-specific certifications and completion of relevant courses, confirming specialized knowledge and expertise.

5. Work Profile Alignment

Beyond qualifications and experience, we carefully analyze the overall work profile to ensure a strong match with the company culture, team dynamics, and long-term career goals. This includes assessing soft skills, communication abilities, and personality traits.

6. Background Checks (where applicable)

Depending on the role and industry, we conduct thorough background checks to ensure candidate suitability and maintain a safe and secure work environment.

7. Skills Assessments

We may utilize skills assessments and practical tests to evaluate technical proficiency and ensure candidates possess the required abilities to perform effectively.

This rigorous process allows us to identify and place top-tier talent, ensuring a successful and mutually beneficial match for both the candidate and the employer.
